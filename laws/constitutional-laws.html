The **Constitution of India** is the supreme law of the country, laying the foundation for governance, fundamental rights, and duties of citizens.

üìú Directive Principles of State Policy (DPSP)

DPSPs (Articles 36-51) are non-enforceable guidelines for governance. Key principles:

  • Economic & Social Justice: Equal pay, fair working conditions.
  • Promotion of Education: Free and compulsory education.
  • Protection of the Environment: Policies to safeguard forests and wildlife.
