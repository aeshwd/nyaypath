üèõÔ∏è Employee Rights Under Labor Laws

  • Right to Fair Wages: No employer can pay below the minimum wage.
  • Right to Safe Working Conditions: Employers must ensure a safe and healthy workplace.
  • Right to Maternity Leave: Female workers get **26 weeks** of paid maternity leave.
  • Right to Provident Fund (PF): Employees get retirement benefits under EPF Act.
  • Right Against Child Labor: No child below 14 years can be employed.
